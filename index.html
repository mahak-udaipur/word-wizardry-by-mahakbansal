HELLO, INTERNET

Hi, I’m Mahak.

I studied engineering and somehow ended up professionally arguing over headlines.

I started out speaking the language of code, logic, and engineering. Somewhere along the way, I realised I was far more interested in the stories behind what people click, remember, share, and feel. So I traded debugging code for debugging headlines, and somehow, that plot twist made perfect sense.

Today, I work where creativity meets strategy. I turn briefs into ideas with a pulse, brand messages into words people might actually want to read, and blank documents into campaigns, captions, blogs, scripts, and stories. I care about the tiny details too, because sometimes one word, one hook, or one perfectly placed comma changes the whole thing.

And when nobody hands me a brief? I write anyway. That is usually when fictional characters arrive with complicated love lives, poetry sneaks into my notes app, and a simple idea quietly becomes an entire world.

OFF THE CLOCK ✦

Big foodie. Music obsessed. Feed me well, pass me the aux, and suddenly I’m writing like deadlines don’t exist.
